Site Name

Win Music

Win Music is a digital platform designed for the music industry. The name reflects the goal of helping artists and music labels grow their audience, promote their work, and generate revenue through digital sales.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a professional online presence for a music industry brand. The platform will allow visitors to explore artists, discover music-related products, and purchase or request information directly through the website.

This project also serves as a learning opportunity to improve my skills in modern web design, layout, and front-end development.

Scenarios

  • Where can I view and purchase music-related products?

    Visitors can browse all available products directly on the website, including artist releases and promotional content.

  • How can I contact the music industry or artists?

    A contact section will be available to allow users to easily reach out for business inquiries, collaborations, or support.

Color Schema

The color palette was selected to create a modern, professional, and music-focused visual identity. Each color has a specific role in improving readability and user experience.
